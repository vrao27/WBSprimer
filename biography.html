RAO

Rao is a multi instrumentalist, guitarist, composer based in Düsseldorf who is active in different genres in music. With a strong musical background in Blues, Jazz and Carnartic, he finds inspiration in trying to find sounds that combine elements from these genres and more.

Born in Bangalore, India into a family of doctors, he decided music was something he could use to make a difference. His first introduction into the world of sound was to carnatic music at a young age. He picked up the guitar at age 16 and has never stopped playing since.

Having study classical guitar in addition to different genres like Rock, Pop and Jazz he began performing with various local bands and went on to create his own band "4th and Main". He eventually moved to Düsseldorf,Germany where he continues to play.
